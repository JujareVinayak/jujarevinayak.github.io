Projects

Push To Talk

Push to talk is a method of conversing on half- duplex communication lines, including two-way radio. Subscription based group communication service that instantly connects team across different networks and locations. Worked on Remote monitoring by which supervisors can keep track of their team.

Order Management App

Orders app is developed to reduce fulfilment time of orders instead of maintaining their records manually. We have saved 30 million hours so far compared to manual records. Orders app is a multi module app build using clean architecture with 100% kotlin. Orders app fulfils multiple shipment types like In Store Pick ups, Locker, Parcel and Delivery. The Fulfillment module covers all of the key tasks, such as claiming any shipments by stamp, sorting, updating staging information, and modifying the order. Order inquiry module is being used to obtain detailed report of order data. Search for any order using the phone number, email address, or order number. Printers module is used to interact with zebra printers. We can obtain order receipts, customer bill prints etc

Remote Park Assit

Remote Park Assit is a mobile app which uses BLE and WiFi to communicate with the car and enables automated car parking for pulling into and out of selected parking spaces. The driver can concentrate on controlling the parking maneuver. He or she has the option to remain in the car or get out ahead of time while the vehicle parks itself. Very useful when needs to be parked in between two cars in tight spaces where you can't open doors and get out of the car.
